PROJECTS

S-VQA: Sentence Based Visual Question Answering

Extended the Visual Question Answering(VQA) task that predicts short answers mainly one word answers to a S-VQA task that generates sentence based answers to a question. Generated relevant S-VQA dataset using natural conversion language rules and implemented a baseline S-VQA model by combining VQA model and LSTM based decoder.

Incremental CLIQUE Algorithm

Developed the incremental version of a grid-based clustering algorithm called CLIQUE. Used for automatic subspace clustering of high-dimensional data for data mining applications

Indian state and UT-wise Enrolment of students in higher education

Visualized the distribution of Gross Enrollment Ratio of students in higher education. Analyzed the general level participation of students in higher education across different states and union territories of India gender-wise and category-wise.
